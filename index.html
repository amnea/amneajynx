Here we go again. It's been a little over 2 years since the last time I published music. I've learned a little more, learned to swallow my pride a little bit and step out of my comfort zone a little more. My social ability is not the best....

For those of you who have never been to this site before check out the "about me" section. I'm on most social media platforms (Twitter, Facebook, etc). I've been there for awhile, looking at my keyboard -- typing then deleting over and over again.

To sum it up: A few years ago I decided the old anti-social me really needed to share the "art" I've been creating for years because art kept in a closet is really just trash you're sentimental about. If no one else experiences it, is it worth anything more that brief therapy session it was?

On August 10th, 2021 Without a Trace will release world-wide. There's more to come but what form or shape that will take I have to stay tight lipped about for the moment...
